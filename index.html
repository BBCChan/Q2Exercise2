Momentum

It describes and object's "quantity of motion
A vector quantity
Its direction is the same as the velocity (v)
The SI unit of momentum is kg m/s

Impluse

The force (f) applied for a period of time (t)
A vector quantity
Its direction is the same as the net force (f)
The SI unit of impulse is N x s

Valence Electrons

Is in the outermost shell of an atom
The very first electrons to come into contact
Needs atleast 8 valence electrons to be stable
